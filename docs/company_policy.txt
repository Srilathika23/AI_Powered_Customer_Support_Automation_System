ABC TECHNOLOGIES - COMPANY POLICY DOCUMENT
==========================================

1. REFUND POLICY
----------------
- Customers are eligible for a full refund within 30 days of purchase.
- Refunds after 30 days are evaluated on a case-by-case basis.
- Refund requests must be submitted through the support portal.
- Approved refunds are processed within 7-10 business days.
- Annual subscription refunds are prorated after the 30-day window.

2. SUBSCRIPTION CANCELLATION POLICY
-------------------------------------
- Customers may cancel their subscription at any time.
- Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing cycle.
- No partial refunds for mid-cycle cancellations unless under 30-day policy.
- Cancellation requests must be confirmed via email.

3. ACCOUNT CLOSURE POLICY
--------------------------
- Account closure requests require identity verification.
- All data is retained for 90 days post-closure before permanent deletion.
- Customers can reactivate accounts within the 90-day retention period.
- Account closure is irreversible after the retention period.

4. COMPENSATION POLICY
-----------------------
- Compensation may be offered for verified service outages exceeding 4 hours.
- Compensation is in the form of service credits, not cash.
- Compensation requests must include outage reference number.
- Maximum compensation is one month of subscription fee.

5. ESCALATION POLICY
---------------------
- Customers may request escalation to management at any time.
- Escalated issues are reviewed within 24 business hours.
- A dedicated case manager is assigned for escalated cases.
- Resolution timeline is communicated within 4 business hours of escalation.

6. DATA PRIVACY POLICY
-----------------------
- Customer data is encrypted at rest and in transit.
- Data is never sold to third parties.
- Customers can request data export at any time.
- GDPR and CCPA compliance is maintained.

7. SERVICE LEVEL AGREEMENT (SLA)
----------------------------------
- Standard support response time: 24 hours.
- Priority support response time: 4 hours.
- Enterprise support response time: 1 hour.
- System uptime guarantee: 99.9% monthly.
