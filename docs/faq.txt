ABC TECHNOLOGIES - FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS (FAQ)
=====================================================

GENERAL QUESTIONS
------------------

Q: What is ABC Technologies software?
A: ABC Technologies provides a cloud-based business management platform that helps companies manage operations, finances, HR, and customer relationships in one unified system.

Q: Is there a free trial?
A: Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial for all plans with no credit card required and full feature access.

Q: Can I change my plan?
A: Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from Settings > Billing > Change Plan.

Q: Is my data secure?
A: Yes. We use AES-256 encryption for data at rest and TLS 1.3 for data in transit. We are SOC 2 Type II certified.

BILLING QUESTIONS
------------------

Q: How do I get an invoice?
A: Invoices are automatically emailed on your billing date. You can also download them from Settings > Billing > Invoice History.

Q: What payment methods are accepted?
A: We accept Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover, and bank transfers (Enterprise only).

Q: How do I request a refund?
A: Submit a refund request through the support portal. Refunds within 30 days are processed automatically; others are reviewed manually within 5 business days.

Q: Will I be charged if I cancel?
A: No charges after cancellation. Your service continues until the end of the current billing period.

ACCOUNT QUESTIONS
------------------

Q: How do I reset my password?
A: Click "Forgot Password" on the login page. A reset link will be emailed to your registered address. The link expires in 24 hours.

Q: How do I update my profile information?
A: Go to Settings > Profile > Edit Profile to update your name, email, phone number, and profile picture.

Q: How do I add or remove users?
A: Navigate to Settings > Team > Manage Users. Only Account Administrators can add or remove users.

Q: How do I activate or deactivate an account?
A: Account Administrators can deactivate user accounts from Settings > Team > Manage Users > select user > Deactivate.

Q: Can I have multiple accounts?
A: Each email address can only be associated with one account. You can be a member of multiple organizations under the same login.

TECHNICAL QUESTIONS
--------------------

Q: Why is my application crashing?
A: Common causes include browser cache issues, unsupported file formats, or files exceeding size limits. Try clearing your cache or using a different browser.

Q: What file formats are supported for upload?
A: PDF, DOCX, XLSX, PNG, JPG, JPEG, CSV, and ZIP files are supported.

Q: What is the maximum file upload size?
A: Starter: 25MB, Professional: 100MB, Enterprise: 500MB per file.

Q: Why can't I log in?
A: Ensure you're using the correct email and password. After 5 failed attempts, the account is locked for 30 minutes. Use "Forgot Password" if needed.

Q: Does the platform work on mobile?
A: Yes, our mobile app is available on iOS (App Store) and Android (Google Play). The web app is also mobile-responsive.

Q: How do I integrate with third-party tools?
A: Go to Settings > Integrations. We support Zapier, Slack, Google Workspace, Microsoft 365, and many more via API.

SUPPORT QUESTIONS
------------------

Q: How do I contact support?
A: Via the support portal, live chat (Professional/Enterprise), or email at support@abctech.com.

Q: What are support hours?
A: Starter: Mon-Fri 9am-5pm EST. Professional: Mon-Fri 8am-8pm EST. Enterprise: 24/7.

Q: How do I escalate an issue?
A: In any support ticket, click "Request Escalation" or ask the support agent to escalate to management.
